    Experience

    Here's what I've been up to

    Apple

    I designed the speakers and microphones in the 2018 MacBook Air.

    UC Berkeley

    I led a research group that investigates flywheel energy storage systems in electric vehicles. I was also a member of the Formula SAE for two years where I designed and validated parts for a racecar suspension system.

    Apple

    As an intern I helped solve an audio performance issue in the 2015 Retina iMacs, and 2016 MacBook Pro with Touch Bar.

    Tesla Motors

    On the Propulsion Systems team at Tesla I solved reliability issues in the motors by redesigning production parts and running validation tests. I also designed a mount and shield to safely measure expansion of rotors during spin testing.

    Lockheed Martin

    As part of the Structures and Mechanisms team for the SBIRS satellite, I drafted several concepts to resolve a critical failure on a contamination door, and developed one into a working model for procurement.

    EnerVault

    I developed the controls and piping layout for an iron-chrome flow battery, and performed flow and leak tests on prototype battery cells. I also contributed to the piping and instrumentation diagrams for the overall system.
